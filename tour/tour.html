AFTER HOURS TIL DAWN

GLOBAL STADIUM TOUR

The After Hours Til Dawn Tour, previously titled The After Hours Tour, is the ongoing seventh concert tour by Canadian singer The Weeknd, in support of his fourth and fifth studio albums, After Hours (2020) and Dawn FM (2022). The tour visited North America, Europe and Latin America between 2022 and 2023 and is set to visit Oceania in 2024.
