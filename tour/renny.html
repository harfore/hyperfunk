RENAISSANCE WORLD TOUR

Beyoncé

The Renaissance World Tour was the ninth concert tour by American singer-songwriter Beyoncé. Her highest-grossing tour to date, it was announced on February 1, 2023, in support of her seventh studio album, Renaissance (2022). The all-stadium concert run began on May 10, 2023, in Stockholm, Sweden, and concluded on October 1, 2023, in Kansas City, Missouri.
